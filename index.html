Are there any legit Fiverr discount codes worth chasing?

Honestly? No. The "discount code" game is mostly dead. Fiverr occasionally runs promos for new users, but they're small potatoes. The real savings come from the strategies I just shared - negotiation, bulk deals, and working the system smart.

Can I game the system with multiple accounts?

Don't even think about it. Fiverr's fraud detection is solid - they'll nuke all your accounts and keep your money. Stick to the legit strategies above - they work better anyway.

How do I spot the good sellers from the time-wasters?

Simple: ignore the 5-star reviews and read the 1-3 star ones. Check their response to criticism. Ask for recent work samples. If they dodge or give generic answers, run. Good sellers are transparent and professional from day one.

What if they deliver garbage?

First: don't accept the delivery. Request revisions immediately. If they refuse or deliver more garbage, escalate to Fiverr support. Document everything - screenshots, chat logs, requirements. Fiverr usually sides with buyers if you have proof.
